- Python scripts:

**Process Management**  
  - 24 process production

**Data Dashboard And Monitor**  
  - Electronic Kanban (Data Dashboard)

  - Production of the Shift
  - Inventory

  - Monthly Production
  - Monthly Scrap Summary
  - Downtime of Equipment
